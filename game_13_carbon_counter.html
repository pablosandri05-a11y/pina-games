What does this mean?

Each AI action uses electricity on massive data-centre servers. Training a large AI model can produce as much CO₂ as five transatlantic flights. A single image generation uses about 10x more energy than a text query.

This doesn't mean AI is bad — it means the choices we make about when and how to use it matter.
