So… why does AI make mistakes?

AI doesn't "understand" images like a person does. It looks for patterns it has seen before. When those patterns are unclear, AI gets confused — just like you did!

🌫️ The image is blurry — patterns are hard to spot
🧩 Part of the image is missing — not enough information
🔄 The angle is unusual — AI has rarely seen this view
📚 AI hasn't been trained on enough examples
❓ The question or image is confusing

🧠 Remember this!

A fast or confident answer from AI doesn't mean it's correct.
Always think critically — even about quick answers!
