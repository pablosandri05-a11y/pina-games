🖥️ AI lives in physical data centres
AI models run on massive racks of computers in data centres — buildings full of servers with specialised chips (like GPUs and TPUs) that process billions of calculations per second. Without those physical machines, AI couldn't run at all.
💡 Key takeaway: "The cloud" is just someone else's data centre. AI does not live in your phone, a USB stick, or a cartoon robot — it lives in real buildings, using real electricity, in real places around the world.
